10. Gujarat Vidyapith

The main aim of the institution was to provide higher education to the people of Gujarat. It also aimed to promote Gandhian values among its students.

The institution was successful in its efforts and produced many notable alumni. Some of the notable alumni of Gujarat Vidyapith include Indulal Yagnik, Morarji Desai, and Kasturba Gandhi.

The institution closed down after the death of Mahatma Gandhi in 1948. However, its legacy continues to this day.
